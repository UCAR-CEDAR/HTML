Joint CEDAR-TIMED Science

Tuesday 18 June 2002
Front Range Theatre
01:00-03:00 pm

Convenor: Scott Palo, U. Colorado Mike Taylor, Utah State

With the recent launch of the TIMED spacecraft, the CEDAR community now has the opportunity to formulate new scientific endeavors. Some of these efforts have been defined via the joint CEDAR-TIMED program. It is the purpose of this workshop to provide ground-based observers within the CEDAR community an opportunity to present their collaborative efforts. Currently two efforts within the CEDAR-TIMED program are planned for this workshop. These are focused on the structure and variability of the semidiurnal tide and gravity waves. If you have a collaborative scientific effort that is relevant to the CEDAR-TIMED program and you would like to present during this workshop, please contact either Scott Palo or Mike Taylor.
