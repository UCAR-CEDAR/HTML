Arecibo Friends Workshop

Sixto Gonzalez (sixto@naic.edu) Arecibo Observatory
03:30-05:30
Thursday, June 20, 2002
Silverthorne

The purpose of this CEDAR workshop is to provide a forum for the Space and Atmospheric Sciences group at Arecibo to interact with our users (and potential users). We will be presenting the latest news regarding

  • the completion of the dual beam upgrade
  • progress of our new datataking system
  • status and highlights of our LIDAR program
  • the future of ionospheric interactions (i.e. HF facility) at Arecibo
  • workshops that we hope to hold on site in the coming year
We invite all to come ready with their questions/comments/complaints and look forward to having a lively discussion.
