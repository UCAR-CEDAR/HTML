TIMED-CEDAR Validation Efforts (GUVI, SABER, SEE, TIDI)

Monday 17 June 2002
Front Range Theatre
03:30-05:30 pm

Convenor:
 Sam Yee
John Hopkins Univ. Applied Physics Lab		 Larry Paxton
John Hopkins Univ. Applied Physics Lab

Kind of Workshop: Campaign planning, preliminary science results

Type of Workshop: Some invited talks followed brief limited talks with lots of discussion.

This workshop will be dedicated to presenting preliminary validation studies of the TIMED instruments and planning collaborations between groundbased and satellite observational teams. The workshop discussions will be lead by representatives from each of the four TIMED instrument teams. We would like to invite all interested to participate in the discussions. For those of you who would like to make short presentations please contact the individual instrument lead

GUVI: Larry Paxton (larry.paxton@jhuapl.edu)

TIDI: Wilbert Skinner (wskinner@umich.edu)

and copy to Sam Yee (240-228-6206).

For more information about the TIMED misssion, go to www.timed.jhuapl.edu and also see our groundbased NSF/CEDAR partners conducting joint TIMED/CEDAR studies.
