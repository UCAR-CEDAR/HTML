2004 CEDAR Workshops

The Monday through Friday workshops were classified into the following categories:

  • ISR = Incoherent Scatter Radar (and science related)
  • OL = Passive Optics and Lidar (and science related to such observations)
  • LDS = Large data set, Multi-instrument array, International campaigns
  • S = Space missions
  • CA = Comparative Aeronomy
Workshops were in:
  • Anasazi Theatre (A, 350 classroom)
  • Sunset Room (S, 130 classroom)
  • Zia Class Room (Zia, 125 classroom)
which were all set for combination classroom and theatre seating.
