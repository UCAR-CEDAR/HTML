Wide-Latitude Substorm Study (WLS)

Convenor: John Foster
Tuesday, June 15 3:25-5:30 PM

The Wide Latitude Substorm Study has organized World Day observing periods twice annually in April and October, beginning in Oct 1996. The focus of these campaigns has been to address the integrated, global ionosphere - thermosphere response to and involvement in the substorm process. Real-time and interactive access to the data from a number of facilities has been accomplished through use of Web-based tools and in collaboration with the SPARC (previously UARC) program. The 1999 WLS intervals form the core of SPARC analysis and education interactive campaigns, and the Septemter 1999 WLS will be run in support of the Intermational Space Weather Month. Event discussion will center on the April 27-29, 1998 WLS campaign observations, during which a number of substorm events were observed in detail (e.g. the event at 23 UT 04/27/98 - 02 UT 04/28/98).

Plans for the 09/99 WLS/SPARC/Space Weather study will be featured.
