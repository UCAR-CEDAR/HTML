Magnetosphere-Ionosphere Mass Transfers and Storm Time Plasmasphere

Conveners:
Dennis Gallagher (dennis.l.gallagher@nasa.gov)
Bob Schunk (schunk@cc.usu.edu)
Fred Menk

2005 Tuesday 28 June 0130-0330 PM

This joint CEDAR/GEM session on ionosphere/plasmasphere coupling was focused on storm-time plasma redistribution, heating, and other mass coupling topics. Several speakers presented their recent results in a relaxed atmosphere, allowing for plenty of question and answer time between talks and discussion at the end of the session. It was felt to be a successful dialogue between the ionospheric and magnetospheric communities regarding mid-latitude/inner-magnetospheric mass coupling.
