2005 CEDAR and GEM Workshops

Final workshop reports for the Joint and the CEDAR workshops are indicated with the words Final Report. There were Guidelines for the 2005 CEDAR Worskhops. The CEDAR and joint reports will be in the Fall 2005 CEDAR Post. Some final reports below have links to .pdfs of individual talks. The GEM and joint reports are in the 2005 September (Vol 15, No. 1) issue of the GEMstone in .pdf form at http://www-ssc.igpp.ucla.edu/gem/GEMstone/GEMstone_Vol15_No1.pdf or accessible at http://www-ssc.igpp.ucla.edu/gem/gemstone.html.
