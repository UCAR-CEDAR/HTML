Distributed and Virtual Observatories

Conveners: From: Mark Moldwin
Mark Moldwin (mmoldwin@igpp.uclaedu), University of California in Los Angeles
Eric Donovan (eric@phys.ucalgary.ca), Univerity of Calgary, Canada

2005 June 29, 0400-0600 PM, Eldorado Zia Room

This joint CEDAR and GEM working group session will share descriptions of ongoing VxO efforts and continue ongoing community discussions of the instrument suites needed for solving some of the long standing solar-heliosphere-magnetosphere-ionosphere-atmosphere coupling problems. What strategy should the DASI community follow: global array of inexpensive instruments or regional array of high-dollar comprehensive systems. What instruments are crucial?
