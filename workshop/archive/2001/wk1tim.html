TIMED/CEDAR

Jeng-Hwa Yee (jeng-hwa_yee@jhuapl.edu) Applied Physics Lab
Stan Solomon (stans@ucar.edu) HAO/NCAR
Front Range, 1:15-3:15
Friday, June 22, 2001

In anticipation of the TIMED launch in the Fall of 2001, the TIMED/CEDAR science team is currently developing science operations plan for the initial phase of the mission. The intercomparison of space and ground measurements, in particular, has been identified as one of the the highest priority tasks. This workshop will be devoted to the observation and team coordinations between TIMED spaceborne instrument teams and groundbased investigators. The workshop will concentrate on the intercomparison of spaceborne (SABER, TIDI, and GUVI ) and groundbased measurements. The TIMED instrument teams will present their validation needs. The goal is to form the intercomparison teams and to develop the observational strategies during the initial phase of the TIMED operations.
